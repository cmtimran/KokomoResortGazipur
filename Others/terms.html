Terms and Conditions

  • All rates are subject to 10% Service Charge and 15% VAT.
  • The above rates include complimentary breakfast for two (2) persons per room. Additional breakfast will be charged at the applicable rates.
  • Check-In time is 1400 hours & Check-out time is 1200 hours. Half Day charge will be applicable for C/O after 1400 hours. Early Check-in and Late Check-out is subjected to a full night charge. (Subject to availability)
  • Usually only two (2) persons are permitted per room. However, if an extra person is to stay BDT. 2000++ (inclusive one extra breakfast) to be charged for an extra bed in limited basis.
  • Guests are advised to reserve rooms in advance. One-night room rent must be paid in advance for the Resort to confirm a room. This advance is non-refundable. Rooms booked without advance will be subject to availability.
  • There is no provision for Drivers and maid. However, we can arrange rooms for Drivers in separate place of the resort as dormitory system (cost approximately Tk. 2000 net full board basis) if we are notified in advance.
  • We are unable to confirm any request of smoking / non-smoking and bed type preferences, which are subject to availability of the preferred room type at the time of check-in.
  • In case of smoking in a non-smoking room, a surcharge of BDT. 2000/= will be applied upon check out.
  • Pets and outside foods are strictly prohibited.
  • Valid Photo ID is a MUST at the time of Check-In.
  • The Reservation & Cancellation policy will be applicable as per Heritage Resort's policy.
