Welcome to Kokomo Sunset Resort

Kokomo Sunset Resort is surrounded by more than 300 coconut trees, and the most mesmerizing greenery. The environment is covered with wonderful nature, along with a gorgeous and clear view of the bright sunset at everyday. If a peaceful vacation full of luxury, with a taste of mother nature is what you’re in search for, The Kokomo Sunset Resort is the perfect place for you to allow an escape from the crowds and noise of the city.
